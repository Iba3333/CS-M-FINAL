Section 1 - Multiple Choice

Select the best answer for each question based on understanding of the concepts.

1. What can a customer service expectations assessment help identify?
📘 Explanation: It can help identify areas of improvement, as well as provide a way of measuring customer satisfaction.
2. Which of the following uses the objective and unbiased third person voice?
📘 Explanation: Publicity uses the objective and unbiased third person voice to report facts.
3. Which of the following comes straight from the seller?
📘 Explanation: Advertising comes straight from the seller.
4. Advertising is characterized as what type of communication?
📘 Explanation: Advertising is characterized as One way communication.
5. Which of the following belongs to public relations?
📘 Explanation: Publicity belongs to public relations.
6. In business markets, compared to consumer markets, purchases:
📘 Explanation: Business Markets Compared to Consumers Markets: Purchases may occur much less frequently.
7. If we change logistics service options, will all customers be affected equally?
📘 Explanation: Not all customers will be affected equally. Some may benefit and some may be disadvantaged.
8. Which marketing strategy targets multiple market segments with unique products, services, or marketing approaches?
📘 Explanation: Differentiated Marketing: The company targets multiple market segments with unique products, services, or marketing approaches.
9. In research terms, what types of research can we undertake?
📘 Explanation: In research terms, we can undertake 'quantitative' research and 'qualitative research'.
10. Which type of research is information provided by others, through reports, surveys or market studies?
📘 Explanation: Secondary Research is information provided by others, through reports, surveys or market studies.
11. Which of the following is an advantage of primary research?
📘 Explanation: Advantages of Primary Research include being Up-to-date, Relevant, Accurate, Reliable, and Confidential.
12. Which of the following is a disadvantage of secondary research?
📘 Explanation: Disadvantages of secondary research include: It may not directly address the research objectives, may be out-of-date, may not be suitable or reliable.
13. Which of the following is an advantage of Focus Groups?
📘 Explanation: Advantages of Focus Groups: Quick, cheap and relatively easy to assemble.
14. Which analysis adds International and Demographic considerations to PESTLE?
📘 Explanation: PESTLIED Analysis includes Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, International, Environmental, and Demographic considerations.
15. Which of Porter's Five Forces examines how easy or difficult it is for new competitors to enter the market?
📘 Explanation: Threat of New Entrants examines how easy or difficult it is for new competitors to enter the market and how much they could disrupt existing businesses.
16. What is the aim of a SWOT analysis?
📘 Explanation: The aim of a SWOT analysis is to convert weaknesses into strengths and threats into opportunities.

Section 3 - Short Answer

Provide a brief written answer for each of the following.

27. What is Customer Service Expectations Assessment?
Model Answer: Customer Service Expectations Assessment is a process of gathering feedback from customers to evaluate whether a company's customer service is meeting the expectations of customers.
28. (Expl) What is the purpose of publicity?
Model Answer: Its purpose is to announce important news. It uses the objective and unbiased third person voice to report facts.
29. What is Mass Marketing?
Model Answer: It is a marketing strategy in which a company promotes a single product or service to a broad and general audience.
30. (Expl) What is the difference between data and information?
Model Answer: Data is 'raw' facts and/or figures, generally numerical. Information is data that has been organized, processed and is presented with context.
31. List three advantages of using Questionnaires.
Model Answer: (Any 3 of the following): Flexibility in delivery, Can cover a large number of people or organizations, Wide geographic coverage, Relatively cheap, No prior arrangements are needed, Respondent have time to think about their responses, No interviewer bias.
32. (Expl) Why is confidentiality required when using diaries for research?
Model Answer: Confidentiality is required as content may be critical.
33. What does PESTLE stand for?
Model Answer: Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Environmental, and Legal.
34. (Expl) Define 'Competition in the industry' in the context of Porter's Five Forces.
Model Answer: It refers to the number of existing competitors and their ability to overperform a company. The larger the number of competitors, the less power a company has.
35. (Expl) What is Price Skimming?
Model Answer: Price Skimming is a strategy where businesses charge a high initial price, and then they lower it over time. It is often used when a new, innovative and/or luxury product is launched in the market.
